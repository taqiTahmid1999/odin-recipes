Beef Stack with just salt and ghee

Description

"Salt and Ghee Beef Stack" is a simple yet indulgent dish that celebrates the natural flavors of beef while enhancing them with the richness of ghee and the subtle seasoning of salt. This dish is a testament to the beauty of minimalism, where a few high-quality ingredients come together to create a truly satisfying culinary experience.

The beef, carefully selected for its tenderness and flavor, forms the heart of this dish. Each slice is generously seasoned with just the right amount of salt, allowing the natural umami flavors of the meat to shine through. Ghee, with its luxurious aroma and buttery richness, adds depth and succulence to every bite, creating a luscious mouthfeel that tantalizes the palate.

As the beef cooks, the ghee melds with the meat, infusing it with a delightful richness and a subtle nutty flavor. The result is a perfectly seared exterior with a tender, juicy interior, creating a harmonious balance of textures and flavors that is sure to leave a lasting impression.

This Salt and Ghee Beef Stack is not just a dish; it's a culinary experience that celebrates the beauty of simplicity and the natural goodness of high-quality ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams beef steak (preferably ribeye or sirloin), thinly sliced
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee

Instruction

  1. Pat the beef slices dry with paper towels to remove any excess moisture. This helps in achieving a better sear.
  2. Season both sides of the beef slices generously with salt, ensuring even distribution.
  3. Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the ghee and allow it to melt and heat up.
  4. Once the ghee is hot, carefully place the seasoned beef slices in the pan, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. You may need to cook the beef in batches depending on the size of your pan.
  5. Sear the beef slices for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop a golden brown crust and reach your desired level of doneness. For medium-rare, aim for an internal temperature of around 130-135°F (55-57°C).
  6. Once cooked to your liking, remove the beef slices from the pan and transfer them to a serving plate or platter.
  7. Allow the beef to rest for a few minutes before serving to allow the juices to redistribute.
  8. Serve the Salt and Ghee Beef Stack hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired, and enjoy the simple yet sublime flavors of this dish.
