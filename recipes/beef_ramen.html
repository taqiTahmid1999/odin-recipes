Ramen with beef

Description

Beef Ramen with Beef Stock is a hearty and comforting dish that combines the rich flavors of beef with the comforting warmth of ramen noodles. This dish brings together tender slices of beef, flavorful beef broth, and an array of fresh vegetables, all simmered together to create a bowl of satisfying goodness. Each spoonful is a symphony of flavors, with the umami-rich beef broth perfectly complementing the tender beef and toothsome ramen noodles. Whether enjoyed as a cozy meal on a chilly day or as a comforting pick-me-up any time of year, Beef Ramen with Beef Stock is sure to warm both body and soul.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams beef (such as flank steak or sirloin), thinly sliced
  • 8 cups beef stock
  • 4 packs ramen noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced (white and green parts separated)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mirin (Japanese sweet rice wine)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms (shiitake or cremini)
  • 2 cups baby spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish

Instruction

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and the white parts of the green onions. Saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the thinly sliced beef to the pot and cook until browned, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Pour in the beef stock, soy sauce, and mirin. Bring the broth to a simmer and let it cook for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. Add the sliced mushrooms and baby spinach to the pot with the beef and broth. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the mushrooms are tender and the spinach has wilted.
  6. Season the broth with salt and pepper to taste. Stir in the sesame oil.
  7. To serve, divide the cooked ramen noodles among serving bowls. Ladle the beef and vegetable broth over the noodles.
  8. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds. Serve hot and enjoy!
